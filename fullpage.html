What did you learn about the system? What happens when ou make the k and m values really large or really small? For really small values of k and m, the mass moves back and forth very slowly - it has a long period, or low frequency. As we increase k and m, the mass moves faster and faster, giving it a shorter period, or higher frequency. What happens when k is big while m is small or vice versa?
